About Me - The Story So Far

I spent the first eighteen years of my life on the island of Kwajalein, a US Army base in the Marshall Islands (about halfway between Hawaii and Australia). Despite what others might view as a somewhat limited lifestyle, I owe much who I am today to those eighteen years on that lovable rock.

The end of my senior year of high school found me packing my bags in search of a new adventure at Stanford University, where I'm currently studying Computer Science and Physics. Every day, I'm struck by the incredible opportunities available to me as I explore Finance, Mathematics, French, Statistics, Game Theory, Cryptography, Socioeconomic Inequality, and so much more. I swing dance, sing, swim, bike, run, row, code, and above all, explore, and I love every minute of it.

My name is John Merriman Sholar, and this is my story so far.
